Fett

This is an example styleguide using Fett, a lightweight styleguide renderer in Javascript. It also serves as the styleguide for my digital resume. Theoretically your own logo would be here, but I'll throw the Fett logo on here as an example.

Styles

Color

These are the primary accent colors, following a monochromatic color scheme for simplicity.

Neutrals like these can be used for most body copy, as they are more readable but draw less attention than accent colors above. A bit more contrast for fonts is okay, such as solid white or black, if they are not on their opposite colors.
